# scheduled.md

Future-dated items that graduate into `workspace/tasks/` when their date arrives.

Tag syntax:
- `- [ ] (YYYY-MM-Wn) item` — week-targeted (graduated by `/week`)
- `- [ ] (YYYY-MM-DD) item` — day-targeted (graduated by `/day`)

Items are moved (not copied) into tasks/ on graduation. Nothing lives in both places.
