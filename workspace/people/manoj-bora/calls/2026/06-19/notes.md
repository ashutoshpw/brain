## Notes — call with Manoj Bora, 2026-06-19

Manoj is building out a paid-acquisition stack for Travysys. He has hired a media marketer who runs ads on both Facebook and Google; the marketer charges an INR 12k/month service fee, and on top of that Manoj is committing INR 30k/month as the actual ad-spend budget — these are two distinct costs.

For his personal Instagram presence, he pays INR 6k/month to a content strategist who both suggests video topics and handles editing. Interestingly, this person also manages her sister's Instagram profile (@shivanginarula.in), which already sits at around 330k followers — a useful signal that she has real-world growth experience.

Manoj also flagged @thekumarmethod as a noteworthy reference: that account reportedly reached 1.2 million Instagram followers from just 6 videos. This is a strong counter-argument against high-frequency posting — quality, targeting, and resonance can far outweigh volume.
