# Overview — health

## Scope

This area covers everything that keeps the body and mind operating at a high level:
- **Fitness** — strength training, cardio, mobility, sport
- **Nutrition** — food quality, macros, meal timing, hydration
- **Sleep** — duration, quality, consistency of sleep schedule
- **Recovery** — active recovery, stress management, HRV trends
- **Medical** — annual check-ups, bloodwork, dental, vision, preventive care

Primary read-only data source: **Whoop** (recovery score, sleep performance, strain, HRV).
All decisions and targets live here; execution happens externally.

## What "good" looks like

- Whoop recovery score ≥ 70 % on ≥ 4 of 7 days per week
- Sleep performance ≥ 80 % (Whoop) on average
- At least 4 structured exercise sessions per week (2 strength + 2 cardio/mobility)
- Resting HR ≤ 60 bpm (7-day rolling average)
- Bloodwork in normal range annually, flagged issues resolved within 90 days

## Key signal

**Whoop weekly recovery average** — a single weekly average ≥ 70 % indicates the body is absorbing training load and stress well. Drop below 60 % for two consecutive weeks = intervention trigger.
