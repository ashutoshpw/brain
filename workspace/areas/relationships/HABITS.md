# Habits — relationships

## Daily

- [ ] Glance at today's important dates (birthdays / anniversaries) in `workspace/people/` and send a message if any match today
- [ ] Respond to any pending messages from close family or friends within 24 hours
- [ ] When someone crosses your mind, send a quick note — don't defer it

## Weekly

- [ ] Sunday relationship check-in: open `workspace/people/` and review all contacts where last contact ≥ (cadence_days − 3); queue messages for the coming week
- [ ] Scan the upcoming week for birthdays / anniversaries (look-ahead 7 days) and prepare personalised messages in advance
- [ ] Send at least one meaningful, non-reactive message to a friend or mentor (not just a reply)
- [ ] Update any `workspace/people/<slug>/profile.md` where something important was learned or where `updated` needs refreshing
- [ ] Log any in-person meet-up or significant conversation in the relevant people profile

## Periodic

- [ ] Monthly: review cadence adherence metric — identify any contacts missed and schedule catch-up (1st of each month)
- [ ] Monthly: add or update at least one people profile (new person or refresh stale `updated` date)
- [ ] Quarterly: send a structured update to each mentor: what you've been building, what you've learned, one ask <!-- TODO: personalize mentors list -->
- [ ] Quarterly: relationships retro — which friendships grew, which faded, what to invest in next quarter
- [ ] Annually: review the people registry for any contacts to archive (moved to acquaintance / inactive) and update cadence targets
