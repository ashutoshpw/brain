# Habits — home

## Daily

- [ ] Process any physical mail / receipts — file, act, or discard on the same day (no "I'll deal with it later" pile)
- [ ] Before bed: quick visual reset of common spaces (30-second tidy — dishes away, surfaces clear)
- [ ] Check tomorrow's calendar for any home/errand items that need prep today

## Weekly

- [ ] Weekly admin block (Saturday 10–11 AM): pay any pending bills, action any document reminders, clear errand backlog, check subscriptions <!-- TODO: personalize time slot -->
- [ ] Review `workspace/areas/home/resources/documents.md` for any renewals due in the next 30 days
- [ ] Check `workspace/scheduled/scheduled.md` for home-related upcoming items and graduate any ready for this week
- [ ] Grocery and household supplies run — check stock against a standard list before going, batch with other errands in the same area
- [ ] Wipe down home office / work surface and ensure it's ready for Monday

## Periodic

- [ ] Monthly: subscription audit — review `resources/subscriptions.md`, flag anything unused in 60 days for cancellation
- [ ] Monthly: bills and renewals check — confirm all recurring payments went through and no document expiry is within 60 days
- [ ] Monthly: maintenance check — scan `resources/maintenance-schedule.md` for anything overdue or due next month
- [ ] Quarterly: declutter session — go room by room with the `resources/declutter/` checklist; donate or discard anything not used in > 1 year
- [ ] Quarterly: update `resources/documents.md` with any new documents acquired and remove expired/replaced ones
- [ ] Annually: full home inventory review — update photos and values in `resources/inventory/` for insurance purposes <!-- TODO: personalize -->
