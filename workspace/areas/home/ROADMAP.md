# Roadmap — home

## Confirmed

1. Create `workspace/areas/home/resources/documents.md` — master document-expiry tracker for passport, insurance, vehicle, and key IDs with expiry dates.
2. Create `workspace/areas/home/resources/subscriptions.md` — full subscription audit with name, cost, billing cycle, last-used date, and keep/cancel verdict.
3. Create `workspace/areas/home/resources/maintenance-schedule.md` — appliances and equipment with last-serviced date and recommended interval.
4. Seed `workspace/scheduled/scheduled.md` with 30-day-lead reminders for every document expiry found in step 1.
5. Establish weekly admin block habit and log adherence in weekly plans (see HABITS.md).

## Proposed candidates

- **[proposed]** `resources/errands-batch.md` — a running list of errands batched by location (grocery area, post office, etc.) to minimise trips. Reviewed weekly during admin block.
- **[proposed]** Quarterly declutter system: a `resources/declutter/YYYY-Qn.md` checklist that goes room by room and tracks items donated / discarded.
- **[proposed]** `resources/emergency-info.md` — a single-file reference for emergency contacts, utility shutoff locations, insurance claim numbers, and property details. Private, not secret (no credentials).
- **[proposed]** Home inventory snapshot: a photo-backed `resources/inventory/` with high-value possessions documented for insurance purposes.
- **[proposed]** Subscription cost dashboard: a simple markdown table in `resources/subscriptions.md` that auto-totals monthly spend — reviewed in monthly finance/home crossover check.
- **[proposed]** `home-pulse` integration (Phase 7): read utility bills or energy data read-only from provider, surface anomalies in METRICS.
