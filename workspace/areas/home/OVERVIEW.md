# Overview — home

## Scope

This area covers everything needed to keep the household running smoothly: recurring admin tasks, document renewals (passport, insurance, subscriptions, vehicle), maintenance schedules for equipment and appliances, errand batching, and decluttering. It is the "life ops" area — not strategic, but essential.

## Current state

- Admin tasks are handled reactively — documents and subscriptions are renewed when they expire rather than proactively. <!-- TODO: personalize -->
- No centralised list of all subscriptions with renewal dates and costs. <!-- TODO: personalize -->
- Home maintenance tasks (appliance servicing, filter replacements, etc.) are done when things break rather than on schedule. <!-- TODO: personalize -->
- Decluttering is infrequent and feels overwhelming because it's never batched or time-boxed. <!-- TODO: personalize -->
- Important documents (passport, insurance, vehicle papers) are stored but their expiry dates are not actively tracked.

## What "good" looks like

- Zero document renewals missed or last-minute: every expiry is surfaced in `workspace/scheduled/scheduled.md` at least 30 days in advance.
- A full subscription audit with costs exists and is reviewed monthly; no zombie subscriptions.
- Home maintenance on a proactive schedule — filter changes, annual servicing, etc. — never reactive.
- A weekly admin block (1 hour) clears all small household tasks before they pile up.
- Quarterly declutter keeps possessions intentional; nothing stored "just in case" for > 1 year.
- The house feels calm and organised 80 % of the time, not just after a big clean.

## Key signal

**Admin tasks cleared on time** — % of recurring home admin items completed by their due date (not deferred into next week). Target: ≥ 90 %.
