# Paid Instagram Growth — Ad Economics

Reference cost model for running a paid Instagram growth engine to market my software products. Sourced from a call with Manoj Bora on 2026-06-19 ([call notes](../../../../people/manoj-bora/calls/2026/06-19/notes.md)), who is running this exact setup for his brand Travysys.

## Monthly cost model

| Component | What it covers | Monthly (INR) |
|---|---|---|
| Media marketer | Runs Facebook + Google ads | 12,000 |
| Ad spend | Actual ad budget (money paid to platforms) | 30,000 |
| Content strategist + video editor | Topic selection + editing for IG videos | 6,000 |
| **Total** | | **48,000** |

Key distinction: the INR 12,000 marketer fee and the INR 30,000 ad spend are separate line items — the marketer is a service fee for managing campaigns, not the ad money itself. Total cash out is INR 48,000/month before results.

## How I'd apply this to my own profile and software products

The goal is a creator-led Instagram funnel that drives software signups and awareness for my products. A few principles before spending anything:

- **Validate the hook and format before scaling ad spend.** Run organic content for 4–6 weeks first. Find what resonates (problem-framing, demo clips, tutorials) before paying INR 30k/month to amplify it.
- **Defer the marketer and editor until month two or three.** Month one can be done with just ad spend on a validated piece of content. Bring in the marketer once I know what to scale, and the editor once the publishing cadence justifies it.
- **The content strategist/editor is the unlock for consistency.** At INR 6k/month, this is the cheapest line item and likely the highest leverage — it removes the "I don't have time to edit" bottleneck that kills most solo-founder content plans.
- **Separate learning budget from scaling budget.** INR 10k–15k/month in initial ad spend is enough to learn which videos drive profile visits and link clicks. Only graduate to the full INR 30k once cost-per-click and conversion signals are clear.
- **Instagram is the top-of-funnel; the product is the bottom.** Every ad should drive to a clear action: follow, link-in-bio click, or DM for a trial. Measure signups per INR spent, not followers.
