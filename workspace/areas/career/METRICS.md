## North-star metric

**Deep-work hours per week** — focused, uninterrupted hours on the highest-leverage task (building, writing, strategy). Target: ≥ 20 h/week on a 4-week rolling average.

## Decision rules tied to metrics

- **Deep-work hours < 12 h/week for 2 consecutive weeks** → audit calendar; remove or delegate lowest-value recurring meetings and tasks.
- **Deep-work hours < 8 h/week** → emergency calendar reset; block first 4 hours of every day as protected deep-work time.
- **No public artefact for 6 consecutive weeks** → schedule a dedicated 2-hour writing block this week; ship something small.
- **Portfolio revenue flat for 3 consecutive months** → run `/go-deep` on the highest-potential brand; review distribution strategy.
- **Consulting hours creeping above ceiling for 3 consecutive weeks** → raise rates or decline new clients to protect product time.
- **No new skill at proficiency after 2 quarters** → evaluate whether current skill is being practiced or whether the roadmap needs revision.
